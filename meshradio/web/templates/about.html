About

MeshRadio is a standalone internet radio that plays the songs people share each day on the Austin MeshCore #music channel, against that day's theme — live as they land, and from a browsable archive of past days.

{% if embed_mode %}

This is the public web player: songs stream from YouTube right in your browser, and your queue is your own.

{% else %}

Running in local mode: this device caches each track's audio and plays it back itself, so the archive keeps working even when the network doesn't.

{% endif %}

Want to contribute to the music channel? The songs come from real people on the Austin MeshCore mesh. Find out how to get on the mesh and join in at austinmesh.org.
