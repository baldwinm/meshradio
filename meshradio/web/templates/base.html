How to use MeshRadio

MeshRadio plays the songs people share each day on the Austin MeshCore #music channel, against that day's theme.

  • Now Playing — ▶ / ⏸ play or pause, ⏭ skip, drag the bar to scrub, and set the volume slider.
  • Queue — songs up next list below. ⤒ plays one next, ✕ removes it, Clear queue empties it.
  • Archive — browse past days and themes. ▶ Play this day replays a whole day; + queue adds a single track.
    • {% if embed_mode %}
  • Playback — songs stream from YouTube right in your browser, and your queue is your own — playing or skipping never affects anyone else.
    • {% else %}
  • Radio — 📻 Start radio keeps the music going with similar songs when the queue runs dry; press it again to stop.
  • Audio — if you see 🔊 Click to enable audio, click it once (browsers block autoplay). Open it in several tabs and only the newest plays sound.
